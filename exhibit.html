2023 Exhibitor Registration

PLEASE NOTE: This is registration ONLY for non-food exhibitors. If you are a food vendor/food truck please email info@goldenvalleypride.com instead and someone will reach out to you.

Registration Steps

  1. Sign up to be an exhibitor by filling out this form.
  2. Prior to payment, Golden Valley Pride must accept your registration via email confirmation. Once you receive confirmation, make your exhibitor payment through this form.
  3. If you’re selling anything in your booth, submit your ST19 form to info@goldenvalleypride.com within 10 days of registering.
  4. Ensure you have a current General Liability insurance.*
  5. After you’ve registered, review the Sponsor and Exhibitor Additional Information packet.

Business/Vendor Booth

  • $150 Eary Bird (through April 30th)
  • $200 Late (May 1st - May 15th)
  • $225 Last Minute (May 16th - event)
    • Includes tent space for for-profit businesses; no additional recognition or benefits.

Community Groups/Nonprofit Booth

  • $100 Eary Bird (through April 30th)
  • $150 Late (May 1st - May 15th)
  • $175 Last Minute (May 16th - event)
    • Includes tent space for non-profit, community groups only at this level.
* NOTE: In order to ensure the accessibility, safety, and security of all participants and attendees, all sponsors and exhibitors who will have a booth at the event must have current General Liability insurance. If you do not have a current policy, you may contact your agent or Pam Peterson at American Family Insurance at ppeters1@amfam.com.
