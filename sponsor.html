Become a Sponsor

The Golden Valley Pride Festival is a great opportunity to get in front of 5,000+ people from Golden Valley and the surrounding area and show them your business or organization values the wonderful diversity of Golden Valley.
Become a sponsor and show your pride!

How to Become a Sponsor

  1. Sign up to be a sponsor by filling out this form.
  2. Make your sponsorship payment through this link.
  3. If you’re selling anything in your booth, submit your ST19 form to info@goldenvalleypride.com within 10 days of registering.
  4. Ensure you have a current General Liability insurance*.
  5. After you’ve registered, review the Sponsor and Exhibitor Additional Information packet.

Platinum: $5,000+

  • Custom event space sponsorship or activity sponsorship
  • Sponsorship announced during Festival programming
  • Logo prominently displayed during Festival
  • Free booth space provided in Platinum Sponsorship Area
  • Organization/business logo on Festival website and social media representation
  • Link to your website

Gold: $2,500 – $4,999.99

  • Sponsorship announced during Festival programming
  • Logo prominently displayed during Festival
  • Free booth space provided in Platinum Sponsorship Area
  • Organization/business logo on Festival website and social media representation
  • Link to your website

Silver: $1,000 - $2,499.99

  • Free booth space provided in Platinum Sponsorship Area
  • Organization/business logo on Festival website and social media representation
  • Link to your website

Bronze: $500 – $999.99

  • Free booth space provided in Platinum Sponsorship Area
  • Organization/business logo on Festival website
  • Link to your website
